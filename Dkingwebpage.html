ADE JULIUS A.K.A OMO BABA

Ade Julius also known as Omo Baba is a Nigerian advance meat seller, who is now the current chairman of Nigerian Meat Sellers Association(NMSA). He own the most famous Abattoir located in Abuja, Nigeria. He's currently the one supplying ASO ROCK, Nigeria Federal house.

  • In 2019, he received an honorary degree in Doctor of Commercial Science from Lagos State University.
  • In 2020, Julius was on the national executive board of the Nigerian Meat Sellers Association(NMSA)
  • He's still serving as a trustee of NMSA
  • He was posthumously inducted into the Junior Achievement Nigeria BusinessHall of Fame in 2021.
